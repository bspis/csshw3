Safety

Spending time in the outdoors can be restful and rejuvenating — so long as you're making smart choices to keep yourself safe. Hover over each of the images below to reveal some tips for ensuring you have the best time possible on your outdoor excursion.

Bring a headlamp or flashlight on your adventure!

Set a time to turnaround so you're back to the trailhead before dark.

Wear coral-safe sun protection.

Make sure you have plenty of food and water for your intended activities.

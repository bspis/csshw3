Why should You Spend Time Outside?

Research shows that spending time outdoors is one of the best things you can do for your physical, spiritual, and mental health. Even spending just five hours in nature can have benefits to your health that last up to a month, including boosting your immune system, lowering your corisol levels, and improving your mood and sleep.

Spending time in nature consistently can reduce your chance of developing heart disease and other conditions. What's more, improving your connection with nature and natural spaces can help reduce feelings of loneliness, isolation, and depression. So what are you waiting for? Go outside and explore our beautiful, amazing, miraculous planet!
